About

A life spent
learning to see.

My name is Sarah, and I am currently a Fine Arts student studying in Dallas, Texas. I have been passionately making art for most of my life and have recently been experimenting with different styles, building a body of work that I'm excited to share.

My paintings range from highly detailed pieces to loose, impressionistic work, with a recent focus on dreamy landscapes and an ongoing interest in portraiture. I'm drawn to the way oil paint holds light — the depth that builds through layers, the way colors shift and breathe.

Based in Dallas, Texas
Medium Oil on canvas
